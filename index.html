    Audio performance

    The sound of science.

    Powered by the Apple-designed H2 chip, AirPods  Pro  3 feature a completely redesigned acoustic architecture that brings breathtaking, all-around-you audio to everything you listen to — from your pump-up playlist to your weekly FaceTime call with family.

    A new multi-port acoustic architecture with more precise airflow control — featuring an inward-facing microphone that points directly into the ear canal — delivers deeper bass, vivid vocal clarity and a wider sound stage, so you never miss a  note.

    A custom-built driver and amplifier work with the H2 chip to create high‑definition, three‑dimensional sound, with super-low distortion during  playback.

    Personalised listening

    For your ears only.

    AirPods  Pro  3 feature next-generation Adaptive EQ, which customises a sound signature for your unique ear geometry and fit. And Personalised Volume uses machine learning to understand your listening patterns and adapt to your preferences over time. 15

    Adaptive EQ tunes music to your ears in real time based on the fit of your AirPods. Inward-facing microphones measure what you’re hearing, then adjust the low to high frequencies of a song — so you get consistently detailed playback, every  time.

    Personalised Spatial  Audio uses the TrueDepth  camera on your iPhone to create a custom profile based on the geometry of your head, delivering deeply immersive sound tailored to  you.

    Dynamic head tracking makes conversations feel like they’re happening in the same room — whether you’re gaming with friends or hosting a Group FaceTime call with  family. 16

    Fitness

    Introducing heart rate sensing.

    All-new heart rate sensing helps you track your heart rate and calories burned during workouts. 3 Using LEDs that pulse invisible light 256 times per second — and sensor fusion from the accelerometers — AirPods  Pro  3 give you incredibly accurate data for up to 50 different workout types in the Fitness app. And with a more secure in-ear fit, you can go all out and they won’t come  out.

    Fitness app. With just AirPods Pro 3 and iPhone, you can dive into the new workout experience in the Fitness app, which includes things like walking, cycling and HIIT. You can set your favourite music and podcasts to play automatically when you start a workout. With the new Apple  Intelligence–enabled Workout Buddy feature, you can get personalised messages that keep you going. 17 And with access to your Move ring — and even the Burn Bar in Fitness+ — you might just never stop.
